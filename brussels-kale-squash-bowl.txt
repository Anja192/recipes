Brussel sprouts, butternut squash and kale bowl

Rice (add sesame seeds when cooked, tamari sauce?), Brussel sprouts roast in coconut oil with garlic splash with balsamic vinegar towards the end and add walnuts (takes about 5 minutes),  butternut squash roasted with thyme, parsley, paprika, salt, pepper, coconut oil. Tear kale up and mix with lemon juice, red onion, tahini, olive oil, tamari, garlic, cayenne, massage into it, the longer you leave it the better it tastes.
